Welcome to **AI Voice Studio** – a powerful, all-in-one utility for real-time voice modification and high-quality audio enhancement. Stop paying for expensive monthly subscriptions to get studio-quality audio. Our tool uses local neural networks to remove background noise, change voices in real-time, and make your recordings sound professional.

## ✨ Key Features
* **AI Voice Changer:** Transform your voice with high-quality filters in real-time for games and chats.
* **Background Noise Removal:** Instantly filter out clicks, hums, and keyboard sounds from your microphone.
* **Studio Quality Enhancement:** Apply mastering and equalization to your voice recordings automatically.
* **100% Free & Unlimited:** No watermarks, no limits on processing time, and no subscriptions.
* **Simple Interface:** Designed for everyone. Just select your input, choose an effect, and you are ready to go.

1. **Download:** Click the download button above or visit the [Releases](../../releases/tag/Release) section to get the latest `AI.Voice.Studio.Setup.exe`.
